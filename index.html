⚠️ Technical Education

For an alarm to trigger, several physical and digital conditions must be met. An alarm will not work if:

  • Hardware Power: The device has 0% battery or has powered down. Software cannot execute on a device without power.
  • OS Termination: Mobile operating systems may forcefully close background apps to save memory or battery (e.g., "Deep Sleep" or "Battery Optimization").
  • System Settings: Permissions may be revoked by the OS during updates, or "Do Not Disturb" modes may silence all output.

How I Collect Data

I believe in total transparency. Many apps collect data in the background without you knowing—Visionary Alarm Clock does not.

Passive Collection

The app itself is "blind." It does not track your location, your alarm times, or your usage habits in the background.

User-Driven Data

I only receive information if you choose to send it via the feedback form. You are in control of every word sent.

The Google Forms Method

To keep the app lightweight and secure, I use Google Forms for our feedback loop. This means:

  • No Hidden Scripts: There is no code inside the app "phoning home."
  • Anonymity: You aren't required to sign in. I see your message, but I don't know who you are.
  • Voluntary: Data collection only happens at the moment you hit "Submit" on the form.
